Welcome to my homepage! I am a 4th-year PhD candidate in the School of Data Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), supervised by Prof. Qi Wu. Before that, I graduated from the Department of Statistics and Finance (Dept. 17) at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and got a Bacholor of Finance. Currently I am available for job interview, and I will get my Data Science PhD degree in 2023, June.

Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of economics & finance (and fintech) and machine learning, with a special focus on causal inference. I am also working on topics such as unsupervised domain adaptation, credit risk analysis, financial time-sires data science, etc.
